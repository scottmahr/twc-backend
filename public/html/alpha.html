Benchmark Workouts

We'll post our benchmark workouts with a brief explanation of what we're trying to find out

  1. 20 min Clean Ladder

    On the top of every other minute, perform a single clean. Start at 70% of your 1RM, and increase weight if possible at every lift.

    This WOD will be used to see how technique changes as you approach your 1RM. Ideally the weight you ramp up to in the last few minutes will be challenging (~95% of 1RM).

    Do not increase weight if your form compromises

  2. The Drain

    Perform 3 rounds for time of:

    • 10 cleans at 75% of your 1RM (hand release at bottom)
    • 500m row
    • 15 burpees
    Close with 10 cleans at 75% of your 1RM (hand release at bottom)

    This WOD will test clean technique as fatigue sets in

    Hand release cleans are necesarry for us to observe a full clean. Wait for the lift status box to turn green before starting your next lift, this should take less than a second after the bar is still.

  3. Hip Hop is Dead

    Perform 5 rounds for time of:

    • 5 cleans at 75% of your 1RM (hand release at bottom)
    • 15 Hip Extensions
    • 10 Toes to Bar
    Close with 5 cleans at 75% of your 1RM (hand release at bottom)

    This WOD will test clean technique as hip fatigue sets in

  4. Hand Release Grace

    Perform 30 reps for time of 135 lb Clean and Jerk (hand release at bottom)

    This is a classic WOD with the hand release aspect. Focus on technique!

FAQ

As we get more questions, we'll expand this section

  1. What is expected of me as an alpha?

    Alpha testers are expected to complete and post at least one of our benchmark workouts per week for the duration of the alpha. For bonus points, this would be accompanied with video footage. Any issues that are encountered during the lifting process should be reported. As an alpha, you help shape our development. We love hearing from you!

  2. How long does the alpha run?

    Our alpha runs for 6 weeks, from August 18 to September 28.

  3. What do I get for completing the alpha?

    Alphas who meet all expectations will get receive a free form collar during our first production run (provided we make it that far!). Also, successeful alphas will get first priority in our beta program.

  4. What is a Hand Release Clean and why are we doing them?

    Hand release cleans are necesarry for us to observe a full clean. Wait for the lift status box to turn green before starting your next lift, this should take less than a second after the bar is still.
